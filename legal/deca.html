Deathcraft Ethical Community Agreement (DECA)

To ensure that you fully understand and agree to the terms, you must carefully read the entire DECA. However, here is a brief summary of the most important points:

  • Behavior: Insulting and inappropriate behavior is prohibited, as is sharing personal data.
  • Privacy: Recording, editing, or sharing audio or video content is reserved for our partners and team members.
  • Legal: Your buildings and creative ideas are entirely your property, but they may be fully used or shared by us and our partners.
  • Submissions: Your submissions remain your property; however, we gain usage rights to the ideas and content that you submit.
  • Data Collection: We collect data regarding your activities, behavior, messages, uploads, and profiles.
